Thank you for your interest in contributing to this cookbook! We welcome recipe contributions and improvements from the community.

## How to Contribute

### Reporting Issues

Issues are **public** and anyone can create them. You don't need special permissions to contribute!

To create an issue:
1. Go to the [Issues tab](https://github.com/boazhachlili/cookbook/issues)
2. Click "New Issue"
3. Choose an appropriate issue template
4. Fill in the required information
5. Submit your issue

### Types of Contributions

You can contribute by:
- **Submitting new recipes** - Share your favorite recipes
- **Improving existing recipes** - Suggest corrections or enhancements
- **Fixing errors** - Report typos or incorrect information
- **Suggesting organization improvements** - Help improve recipe categorization

### Recipe Format

All recipes in this cookbook follow a consistent format. When suggesting a new recipe, please include:

```json
{
  "section": "category name",
  "title": "Recipe Title",
  "language": "he/en",
  "diet": "vegan/vegetarian/meat/fish",
  "equipment": ["list", "of", "equipment"],
  "description": "Brief description of the dish",
  "ingredients": "List of ingredients with measurements",
  "steps": [
    "Step 1",
    "Step 2",
    "Step 3"
  ],
  "tips": [
    "Optional cooking tips"
  ]
}
```

### Language Policy

The cookbook maintains content in its original language (Hebrew or English). No translations are required.

### Dietary Categorization Rules

- Recipes containing eggs or dairy are marked as **vegetarian**, not vegan
- This follows the established policy documented in the cookbook
